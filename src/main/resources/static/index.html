Welcome to CarryMyStuff

A simple platform that connects people who need to move stuff with helpers who have vehicles.

What can you do?

  • Users can find active vehicles and book a time slot.
  • Helpers can see all bookings made with their vehicles.
  • Admins can manage all bookings in the system.

How it works

  1. Register as USER or HELPER.
  2. Log in with your account.
  3. As a USER: see active vehicles and create bookings.
    As a HELPER: see the bookings where you are the helper.
  4. Admin accounts are created manually.
